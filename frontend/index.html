Let's Build Your Plan

Answer 7 quick questions — we'll generate a structured programme and sync it to your devices.

Progress1 of 7
Question 01 / 08
What are you training for?
Pick the distance you're building towards — this sets your sessions, paces, and build phases.
Question 02 / 08
How far have you run lately?
Think about the last few weeks — your longest recent run sets your week-one long run.
Question 03 / 08
Which days can you run?
Select every day that works for you — we'll fit your sessions around them.
Question 04 / 08
Which day is your long run day?
The long run is the anchor of your week — usually a relaxed weekend morning when you have time.
Question 05 / 08
How many days a week do you run right now?
Most runners improve fastest by adding just one more day per week to what they're already doing.
Question 06 / 08
What's a recent race time?
Enter a real finish time from any recent race or time trial — we'll use it to set personalised training paces. Not sure? Skip it.
